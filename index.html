How to use Earthquake Tracker

Welcome to Earthquake Tracker! This website allows you to search for recent earthquakes around the world and view them on a map. Here's how to use it:

  1. Enter a location in the search bar and click "Search".
  2. Adjust the search parameters as desired, and click "Submit".
  3. The earthquake results will be displayed in a list on the left, and a map with markers for each earthquake will be displayed on the right.
  4. Click on an earthquake in the list or on a marker on the map to view more details about that earthquake.
  5. Use the zoom and pan controls on the map to explore different areas.
